Blitzle Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Blitzle respects your privacy. This game does not directly collect, store, or sell personal information.

Gameplay Data

Blitzle stores certain gameplay information locally on your device, including settings, scores, and progress.

Google Play Games Services

Blitzle uses Google Play Games Services for leaderboards and player sign-in.

When using these features, your Google Play Games profile information and leaderboard scores may be processed by Google according to Google's own privacy policies.

Blitzle does not have direct access to your password, email contents, payment information, or other sensitive Google account data.

Data Collection

Blitzle does not directly collect, store or share:

  • Passwords
  • Payment information
  • Private messages
  • Contacts
  • Precise location data

Third-Party Services

Blitzle may rely on third-party services provided by Google Play Games Services. These services may process limited account and gameplay data required for authentication, achievements, and leaderboard functionality.

You can learn more about Google's privacy practices here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future.
